The investment bank's shift reflects a strategic pivot toward mainland China's artificial intelligence hardware ecosystem, where Goldman sees stronger near-term growth catalysts tied to domestic AI infrastructure buildout. The move comes as the Hang Seng Index fell -1.56% overnight, underperforming the Nikkei's +2.50% gain and highlighting the divergence in Asian equity performance.
McDonald's unveils new growth strategy to win back customers
McDonald's announced its new 'NEXT' growth strategy, a comprehensive plan designed to reverse recent traffic declines and rebuild its customer base through a combination of automation, improved food quality, enhanced social media marketing, and elevated in-store hospitality standards. The fast food giant outlined plans to accelerate technology integration across its restaurant network, including automated ordering and kitchen systems aimed at improving speed and consistency. The strategy represents McDonald's most significant operational overhaul in recent years as the company faces intensifying competition from fast-casual rivals and ongoing consumer sensitivity to menu pricing.
