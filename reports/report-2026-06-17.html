3 things to know about the new Fed chief's first meeting
Kevin Warsh is chairing his first Federal Reserve policy meeting since taking over as Fed Chairman, with the central bank widely expected to hold interest rates steady. Warsh, a former Fed governor, brings a market-oriented perspective to the role and faces close scrutiny over how he will communicate the Fed's policy path. His post-meeting press conference marks the first time he will take questions from reporters in his new capacity, making his remarks a key focus for markets.
