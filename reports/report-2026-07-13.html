Meta expands Louisiana data center in $50B AI push, boosting rural community
Meta announced a $50 billion expansion of its data center in Richland Parish, Louisiana, a move that would make the facility one of the largest data centers ever built. The investment is part of Meta's broader push to scale its artificial intelligence infrastructure. The project is expected to bring new jobs and additional funding to the surrounding rural community.
Strait of Hormuz traffic lowest in 5 weeks
Ship traffic through the Strait of Hormuz fell to its lowest level in five weeks on Sunday, with trade intelligence firm Kpler recording just six vessels passing through the waterway. The Strait of Hormuz is one of the world's most critical chokepoints for oil and gas shipments, making any sustained reduction in traffic a closely watched indicator for global energy markets. The decline coincides with escalating US-Iran tensions in the region.
SK Hynix stock falls, leads broader chip sector declines as AI trade angst returns
SK Hynix shares declined sharply, dragging the broader semiconductor sector lower as renewed uncertainty around the AI trade weighed on chipmakers. The company has been a major beneficiary of surging demand for high-bandwidth memory used in AI applications, making it particularly sensitive to shifts in AI investment sentiment. The pullback reflects growing caution among investors about the pace and durability of AI-driven chip demand.
