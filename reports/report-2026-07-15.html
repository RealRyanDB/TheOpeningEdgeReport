The energy sector encompasses companies engaged in the production, exploration, refining, and transportation of consumable fuels including coal, oil, and natural gas. The sector remains a closely watched segment of the market given its sensitivity to commodity prices and global supply dynamics.
Inflation slowed sharply -- but it may not last
Annual inflation eased to 3.5% in June, pulling back from May's more than three-year high and offering a measure of relief to consumers and policymakers. The resumption of conflict with Iran has introduced fresh upward pressure on energy costs, which could reverse some of that progress in the months ahead. The competing forces of cooling domestic inflation and rising geopolitical risk leave the near-term price outlook uncertain.
'Arsenal of democracy': Jamie Dimon announces $24 million effort to boost American shipbuilding
JPMorgan Chase CEO Jamie Dimon announced a $24 million initiative aimed at strengthening American shipbuilding capacity, with funding directed toward a new submarine facility at the Philadelphia Navy Yard. Dimon framed the effort as part of a broader commitment to domestic industrial investment, invoking the phrase 'arsenal of democracy' to underscore the national security dimension of the initiative. The announcement positions JPMorgan as a significant private-sector participant in efforts to rebuild U.S. maritime manufacturing infrastructure.
Watch live: Vought testifies before House on CFPB
Office of Management and Budget Director Russ Vought appeared before the House Financial Services Committee on Wednesday morning to present the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's semi-annual report. Since assuming leadership of the CFPB, Vought has moved aggressively to curtail the agency's operations, including implementing funding freezes and restricting staff from conducting investigations or engaging in enforcement activities. The hearing represents a significant moment of congressional oversight over the administration's approach to the consumer finance regulator.
